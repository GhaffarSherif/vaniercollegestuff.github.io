Page Description

Home

Welcome to the Homepage. This page is meant to welcome visitors and contains a short description of each page and more information of the developers.

Fun

Fun page was the biggest challange for us and was the orginal idea for this website. Using javascript, we wanted to replicate the EelSlap website. The idea is that your mouse cursor/arrow keys would change the frames of a slow motion GIF and the effect would make it seem like the user is in control of the outcome of that particular GIF.

Games

Everyone loves games. When you're feeling stressed or just bored, you can always drop by our games page. We offer four classic games that you can enjoy. These games are coded using javascript and can be acessed offline when loaded.

GIFs

Lets face it, the internet is all about cats. Our GIFs page offer funny and cute gifs that we post. These gifs are loaded with AJAX which makes it much easier to add/update new gifs.

Contact US

We offer all our visitors an oppurtunity to send us comments and questions that they may have at the contact us page. This page will verify certain information is stored in a cookie and that the inforation is in fact valid.

FAQ

As a quick guide and to further help visitors understand and feel more comfortable with the website, we added a Frequently Asked Questions page. This page will contain top 10 selected questions that can be navigated with a table of contents (dynamic text). Also another section will offer users to send us a question by filling out a form which when submitted alows the user to email it to us
